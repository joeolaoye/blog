I'm **Joseph Olaoye** — a technology leader who builds and ships.

Over the last decade I've been lucky to sit at the intersection of **engineering leadership**, **payments infrastructure**, and **0→1 product building** across Africa and beyond.

## What I've done

- **Director of Engineering / CTO at [Kora](https://korahq.com)** — led multiple engineering teams and managers, moving the platform from early-stage to serving pan-African merchants at scale.
- **Founding engineering at [Flutterwave](https://flutterwave.com) and [Korapay](https://korapay.com)** — part of the teams that built payments infrastructure now processing billions in volume, backing thousands of African businesses.
- **Shipping products that power payments for millions of users** across multiple regions, working with teams distributed across the US, Africa, and Europe.

## What I'm focused on now

- **AI-native products** — how businesses rebuild their core workflows around agents, not just chat.
- **Lean, scalable systems** — small teams, heavy automation, short feedback loops.
- **[tjoc.dev](https://tjoc.dev)** — my current workshop for MVPs, agentic systems, and 0→1 software.

## How I work

I care about **correct primitives**, **short paths to production**, and **engineers who can own outcomes** — not just tickets. I write about the boring, load-bearing parts of building software: security, architecture, hiring, and what actually ships.

## Working with me

I'm always open to conversations about bold ideas, early-stage products, and AI-native companies.
